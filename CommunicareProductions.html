About Communicare Productions

At Communicare, we’re dedicated to bringing the stories of organizations to life in ways that resonate with stakeholders, clients, and communities. We understand that every organization has a unique mission, and our goal is to create films that authentically communicate not only what you do, but why it matters.

Our Approach

We believe in close collaboration, taking the time to understand your vision, goals, and values. By blending creativity with a human-centric perspective, we create narratives that:

  • Reflect authenticity.
  • Advance inclusive agendas.
  • Inspire systemic change.

    • Through the synergy between Communicare and Search Ends Inclusion and Impact, we ensure that every story we tell reflects the lived realities of the communities we serve.

    Our Service

    What we offer?

    Sensitization and Awareness Campaigns

  • Short films and social media content highlighting real stories from marginalized groups.
  • Emotionally impactful visual content for DEI workshops, leaving a lasting impression.

    • Certification Programs and Talent Advocacy

  • Mini-documentaries celebrating organizations’ journeys toward DEI certification.
  • Campaigns showcasing diverse talent pools, inspiring inclusive recruitment practices.

    • Confidential Conversations Awareness

  • Videos promoting the importance of counseling services for employees, emphasizing inclusive and supportive workplace environments.

    • Feature-Length Impact Stories

  • Documentaries showcasing the challenges and triumphs of fostering inclusive workplaces.
  • Personalized storytelling to highlight leadership and lived experiences within the DEI framework.

    Our Vision and Mision

    Vision

    To craft authentic, meaningful stories that inspire action, elevate underrepresented voices, and create a lasting social impact.

    Mission

    By blending creativity with conscience, we leverage the transformative power of audio-visual media to highlight human experiences, amplify organizational missions, and advocate for equity and inclusion.

    Kanu Bharti
    MD and CEO, Search Ends

    Kanu is a traveler, a writer and an obsessive story teller. He has been a film director and a creative producer in visual arts for two decades now. His continuous search for a story takes him to various parts of the country and the world. His travels are a string of vibrant conversations with people he meets.

    Kanu founded Communicare, a production company managed by team of high energy, creative, hands- on professionals. Communicare works with Companies, civil society groups and other institutions to strategize and build their communication programs through various mediums - conventional and experimental. He has worked on 70 films and has independently directed more than 50 films under his banner Communicare. Kanu lives out of a suitcase between Ahmedabad, Udaipur, Delhi and wherever his work takes him. He loves new challenges and exploring new platforms to tell his stories.

    Born with an innate curiosity and a deep appreciation for the art of filmmaking, Aditya's journey in the industry began with a sincere love for cinema. Over the years, he has honed his skills and developed a keen eye for capturing moments that transcend the screen. His work reflects a perfect blend of technical precision and artistic finesse, making him a sought- after professional in the world of photography and editing.

    Rajul Bharti
    MD Searchends inclusion Impact

    Rajul is a seasoned development communications professional with a career spanning over 18 years. With a master's degree in mass communication from Maharaja Sayajirao University, Baroda, and a bachelor's in psychology from the same institution, coupled with a diploma in Theatre from Music College, Rajul has cultivated a diverse skill set that merges the realms of communication, psychology, and artistic expression.
